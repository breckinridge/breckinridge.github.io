Why This Group Was Created

On April 20, 2015, Dean John Boyer and Vice President Karen Warren Coleman announced the closure of five dorms and the retirement of nine house names. As a response to these actions, Save Our Satellites UChicago was formed and a student committee was elected with the intention of working to ensure diversity of housing options on campus.

Our Purpose

To fight for the ability of specific dorms to remain open. To guarantee the preservation of the affected houses’ current names. To ensure cultural, geographic, and structural diversity of dorms for future generations of UChicago students.
